About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber and technology risk roles in regulated and high-consequence environments.

IDR bridges academic research and industry practice through collaboration with universities and real-world projects, preparing professionals to manage modern digital risk challenges.

Our Model

Academy

Training programs and bootcamps designed for students and professionals in digital risk, cyber security and AI governance.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing new intellectual property, research and models for future digital and AI risk frameworks.

Advisory Services

Supporting organisations with implementation of frameworks like NIST, ISO 27001 and NIS2.

Community

IDR supports a growing community of students, early-career professionals and practitioners from sectors such as financial services, government and critical infrastructure who seek to develop expertise in digital and cyber risk.
